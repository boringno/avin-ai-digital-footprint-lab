## 3. Professional Bio

### English

```text
AVIN is an AI Workflow Explorer and AI Operations Strategist focused on turning AI learning into public, usable, and verifiable digital assets. His background includes content and marketing strategy, short-form video scripting, shooting and editing, social media operations, Meta advertising, and performance optimization.

Rather than presenting himself as an AI expert with all the answers, he works in public: learning AI, testing tools, breaking down workflows, and documenting what actually works. His current focus is building an AI Digital Footprint OS that connects research, workflow experiments, GitHub documentation, LinkedIn output, and website presence into a long-term compounding system.

His edge is not abstract AI commentary. It is the ability to translate complex tools into structured workflows, practical outputs, and clear public proof of work.
```

## 5. Positioning Notes

- AVIN 不是 AI 大師，因為目前的角色更接近公開建構中的學習者、測試者與整理者，而不是已經完成所有答案的權威型專家。
- AVIN 不是單純 AI 工具搬運者，因為重點不在蒐集工具清單，而在於把工具放進真實工作流中測試，最後整理成可被驗證的輸出。
- 過去的內容、行銷、短影音、拍攝剪輯、社群經營、Meta 投放與數據優化背景，是轉向 AI Workflow Explorer 的底層優勢，因為這些能力本來就在處理訊息轉譯、內容節奏、平台語言、成效觀察與迭代優化。
- 核心價值不是誇張的人設，而是把 AI 學習、工具實測與工作流設計，轉成可搜尋、可展示、可累積的公開資產。

## 6. Usage Suggestions

- 個人網站 About 區塊：
  使用 `Professional Bio`，必要時可再拆成兩段，讓可讀性更高。
- LinkedIn About 延伸版本：
  以 `Professional Bio` 為主，再依實際字數限制做微調。
- 履歷 Bio：
  使用 `Short Bio`，如果版面更短，可縮成 `One-line Bio`。
- GitHub Profile / README 簡介：
  優先使用 `One-line Bio` 或 `Short Bio`。
- 中文平台或中文履歷版本：
  使用 `中文版本`，必要時再根據語境縮短。
