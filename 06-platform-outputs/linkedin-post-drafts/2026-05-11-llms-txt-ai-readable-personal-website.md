## 1. LinkedIn Post Draft - English

```text
Personal websites are no longer only for human readers.

AI systems and LLM-based search tools may also become part of how people discover and understand our work.

That is one reason I added a public `llms.txt` file to my personal website.

Alongside the GitHub Pages homepage, it gives AI systems a lightweight way to understand what this project is, who I am, how the public structure is organized, and how the GitHub repo, LinkedIn profile, and website connect together.

For me, this is part of building AVIN AI Digital Footprint OS in public.

The goal is not to make a flashy AI landing page. The goal is to turn AI learning, workflow experiments, and content strategy into searchable, verifiable, and AI-readable public assets.

I think this will matter more as AI-assisted browsing, summarization, and search keep becoming part of how people navigate the web.

Website:
https://boringno.github.io/avin-ai-digital-footprint-lab/

llms.txt:
https://boringno.github.io/avin-ai-digital-footprint-lab/llms.txt
```

## 2. LinkedIn Post Draft - Chinese

```text
個人網站可能不再只是給人看的頁面。

未來 AI 搜尋、LLM 摘要、AI 助理，也可能成為網站的另一種讀者。

這也是我這次在個人網站裡加入 `llms.txt` 的原因。

除了 GitHub Pages 首頁，我也把一份公開可讀的 `llms.txt` 一起放上去，讓 AI 系統能更快理解這個網站是什麼、AVIN 是誰、這個公開專案的結構是什麼，以及 GitHub、LinkedIn、Website 之間如何連動。

對我來說，這不是多放一個檔案而已，而是 AI Digital Footprint OS 的一部分。

我想做的不是把網站包裝成 AI 大師頁，而是把 AI 學習、工具實測、工作流拆解與內容策略，整理成可搜尋、可驗證、也能被 AI 讀懂的公開資產。

如果未來 AI 會越來越常參與「理解網站」這件事，那我們也許該開始思考：網站不只要對人清楚，也要對 AI 清楚。
```
