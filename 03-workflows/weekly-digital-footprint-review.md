# Weekly Digital Footprint Review

## Goal

Review whether weekly AI learning and content activity is creating durable public assets.

## Workflow

1. List what was learned this week.
2. Count what became notes, prompts, workflows, or public posts.
3. Review which assets are strongest by clarity, reuse, and credibility.
4. Identify missing conversions:
   learning that never became an asset, or assets that never became public outputs.
5. Select one item to refine next week.
6. Record lessons about what formats are compounding best.

## Weekly Questions

- What did I learn
- What became reusable
- What became public
- What is still trapped in raw notes
- Which assets improved my digital footprint

## Output

- one weekly review note
- one priority list for the next week

## Success Standard

A good week is not measured only by how much was consumed.

It is measured by how much was turned into durable, visible, reusable work.
