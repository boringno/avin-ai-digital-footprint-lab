# Notion To LinkedIn

## Goal

Turn a raw note or idea captured in Notion into a credible LinkedIn post draft.

## Workflow

1. Capture an idea, experiment, or learning note in Notion.
2. Extract the single most useful takeaway.
3. Rewrite the note around one audience problem.
4. Use the credibility review skill to remove inflated claims.
5. Format the final version into a short LinkedIn structure:
   hook, context, insight, takeaway.
6. Publish or queue for scheduling.

## Inputs

- raw Notion note
- audience
- publication goal

## Output

- one publish-ready LinkedIn post

## Review Questions

- Is the post about a real lesson instead of a vague opinion
- Does it contain one clear insight
- Can a reader learn something without extra context
